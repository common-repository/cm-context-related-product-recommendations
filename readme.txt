== Description ==

[Product Page](https://www.cminds.com/wordpress-plugins-library/custom-product-recommendations-for-wordpress/) | [Videos](https://www.videolessonsplugin.com/video-lesson/lesson/wp-contextual-product-recommendations/)  | [Demo](http://listwp.com/hiking-product-recommendation-demo/) | [User Guide](https://creativeminds.helpscoutdocs.com/category/446-contextual-product-recommendations-cmcpr) 

**Easily display recommended products, both local and external, based on the content that appears on your website's pages or posts.**

Product Recommendation plugin simplifies the process of referring readers to related products by automatically generating recommendation based on the content mentioned on each page or post. 

Users can utilize this plugin to cross-sell their products or earn affiliate commissions.

The product recommendation appears as a widget featuring several related products displayed as images or text, with each product linked to its respective page on your WordPress site or an external site. A

dditionally, each term associated with the product within the post can be linked to the external product for easy access.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Define your  terms under the plugin menu item in the administration interface.  The title of the page should be the term.  The body content should be the definition.
4. There is a handful of other optional preferences available in the dashboard.
